Top Attractions and Experiences

Explore the Rich Heritage

Changanassery offers an array of experiences that reflect its vibrant cultural heritage. From ancient marketplaces to majestic religious sites, every corner of the town tells a story.

St. Mary's Metropolitan Cathedral

A historic church that blends European and traditional Kerala architectural elements, this cathedral is one of the town’s most revered landmarks.

Learn More
Vazhappally Maha Siva Temple

One of Kerala’s oldest temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, renowned for its intricate wood carvings and authentic Kerala style architecture.

Learn More
Lakshmipuram Palace

This regal palace, once home to the local royal family, offers a fascinating glimpse into Changanassery’s illustrious past.
