About Changanassery

Changanassery, located in the Kottayam district of Kerala, India, is a town steeped in history and cultural heritage. Often known as the "City of Five Lamps" (Anchu Vilakku), it has been a vibrant center for trade, education, and religious harmony for centuries.

From ancient marketplaces and regal palaces to timeless temples and majestic churches, Changanassery offers visitors a unique glimpse into the soul of Kerala. Explore the town’s storied past, diverse cultural landmarks, and scenic beauty that continues to enchant travelers.
